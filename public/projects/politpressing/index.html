Politpressing.org

Public database of political criminal cases and persecutions in current Russia.

  • Overview
  • Concepts
  • In press

PolitPressing.Org is to monitor political repressions in Russia.

By political repressions - or politpressing - we means the persecution of people for political reasons on the part of the state.

The objective of the project - gathering and promotion of information about the persecution. We want:

  • to bring together all available data on the political persecutions in modern Russia
  • to make this data accessible and understandable to a wide audience
  • provide data collected handy tool for further professional work with them

We believe that the availability of information leads to changes, and we hope that the data we collect, analyze and publish, will be actively used by journalists, lawyers, human rights activists and researchers to political repression have gone down in history.
